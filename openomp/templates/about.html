What is OpenOMP?

OpenOMP is an open-source online market platform that can be used to quickly create niche online markets.

Niche markets are usually organized through specialized forums. OpenOMP helps niche markets to grow by making it more accessible than obscure forums.

This website is a demonstration of an example application of OpenOMP. The OpenOMP platform can be used for a variety of niche markets (e.g. knive makers, board game creators, wooden decorative items,... ).
